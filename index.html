Why find a mentor?

Guidance
They’ve been there and done whatever goal you have set for yourself. Your mentor may notice potential in you that you might not see in yourself.
Opportunity
Mentors on Mentuit have wide networks of contacts and connections. Having a mentor opens up doors that you otherwise would not have access to.

Why be a mentor?

Impact
Mentees will look up to you and your experience, and you will have influence on their career and life outlook. Enlightening another person’s life can also help you redefine your own career path and goals!
Self
Strengthen the lessons you’ve already learned. The life you end up changing could be your own. Mentorship on Mentuit isn’t a one-way process. It’s a two-way street. It has the potential to change both sides of the equation.
